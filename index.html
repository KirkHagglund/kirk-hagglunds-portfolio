About Me

Hi there! My name is Kirk Hagglund, thanks for visiting my coding portfolio. I'm originally from Toronto, but currently based in Costa Rica. Previously, I was heavily involved in the Toronto restaurant industry, working as a frontline service provider, a Sommelier and wine program director, and mid level manager at several reputable and fast paced destinations. Like many people, during the pandemic I had much more time to foster hobbies and interests, where I rediscovered my love for puzzles and problem solivng. This led me to enroll in a coding bootcamp administered by UTSCS. With the tools I learned there, I've begun to create my own websites and applications, which I would be honoured to have you explore to see if we might be a good fit professionally. Enjoy!
